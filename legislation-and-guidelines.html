legislation & guidelines

Over 20 Acts come under the purview of the Ministry of Health. These Acts regulate public health and safety, including the healthcare profession, healthcare practices / establishments as well as statutory boards charged with these responsibilities

  • (25KB)

An Act to regulate the manufacture, import, supply, presentation and advertisement of health products and of active ingredients used in the manufacture of health products.

category: legislation

date: 1st Nov 2016

  • dental registration act

An Act to establish the Singapore Dental Council, to provide for the registration of dentists and oral health therapists.

category: legislation

date: 1st Nov 2016

  • poisons act

Act to regulate the importation, possession, manufacture, compounding, storage, transport and sale of poisons.

category: legislation

date: 1st Nov 2016

  • biological agents and toxins act

An Act to prohibit or Cotherwise regulate the possession, use, import, transhipment, transfer and transportation of biological agents, inactivated biological agents and toxins, to provide for safe practices in the handling of such biological agents and toxins.

category: legislation

date: 1st Nov 2016

  • health promotion board act

An Act to establish the Health Promotion Board, to provide for its functions and powers.

category: legislation

date: 1st Nov 2016

  • infectious disease act

An Act relating to quarantine and the prevention of infectious diseases.

category: legislation

date: 1st Nov 2016

  • Advance Medical Directive Act

An Act to provide for, and give legal effect to, advance directives to medical practitioners against artificial prolongation of the dying process

category: legislation

date: 1st Nov 2016

  • private hospitals and medical clinics act

An Act to provide for the control, licensing and inspection of private hospitals, medical clinics, clinical laboratories and healthcare establishments.

category: legislation

date: 1st Nov 2016
