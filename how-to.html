step 1

Download the CHAS Application Form

You may also pick up a CHAS application form at any of the following locations:

  • - Public Hospital OR
  • - Polyclinic OR
  • - Community Centre and Club (CC) OR
  • - Community Development Council (CDC)

step 2

Sign & Send The Completed Form

  • - Send all forms to P.O. Box 680, Bukit Merah Central Post Office, Singapore 911536
  • - For families with foreigners, please include copies of your foreign identification documents (e.g.work permit/long-term visit pass)

step 3

Receive Your CHAS Card

  • - Successful applicants will receive either a blue or orange Health Assist card, depending on the subsidy tier you are eligible for.
  • - All eligible family members will also receive the card.
  • - Each card is valid for 2 years & is non-transferrable
