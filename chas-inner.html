Community Health Assistance Scheme (CHAS)

OVERVIEW

The Community Health Assist Scheme (CHAS) enables Singapore Citizens from lower- to-middle income households, as well as all Pioneers to receive subsidies for medical and dental care at participating General Practitioner (GP) and dental clinics.

Who is eligible?
  1. Singapore Citizens who fulfill the following:

    • households with income, household monthly income per person must be $1,800 and below

    • households without income, Annual Value (AV1) of residence as reflected on the NRIC must be $21,000 and below

  2. Pioneers & Citizens on Public Assistance

Benefits of CHAS

Singapore Citizens who qualify for CHAS will receive an individual blue or orange Health Assist card. With the Health Assist card, they can receive subsidies at CHAS GP and dental clinics for common illnesses, selected chronic conditions and selected dental services. CHAS clinics may be identified by the CHAS sticker displayed at their entrances.

Cardholders will also enjoy subsidised referrals to:

  • Specialist Outpatient Clinics (SOCs) located at Public Hospitals
  • National Dental Centre (for dental referrals)
  • Specific Community Health Centres (CHC)
