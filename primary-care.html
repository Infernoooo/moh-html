Interim Disability Assistance Programme for the Elderly (CHAS)

This scheme is for seniors who did not qualify for ElderShield

Benefits

  • Monthly payouts up to S$250/month

Eligibility

  • Unable to perform 3 or more Activities of Daily Living (ADLs)
